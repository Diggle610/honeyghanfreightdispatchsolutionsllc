About Our Mission

HFDS helps owner-operators and small fleets maximize revenue with dependable dispatch services and personalized logistics support.

At Honeyghan Freight Dispatch Solutions LLC, we treat your truck like it's our own. We know the realities of the road—fluctuating market rates, volatile fuel costs, and stressful back-office workloads. That's why we established a logistics framework tailored to give truckers complete load operational transparency.

Whether you are running regional box trucks or heavy-haul regional flatbeds, our experienced agents stay on the line to monitor your profit parameters, protect your authority profile, and optimize scheduling.

The HFDS Core Commitment

  • ✓ No Forced Dispatch: You maintain control over your asset footprint. You run lanes you choose.
  • ✓ Broker Credit Protection: We verify all broker lines before loading up your trucks.
  • ✓ Dedicated Desk: Real 1-on-1 human dispatchers who pick up when you ring.
