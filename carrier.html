Onboarding Checklist

To get your equipment loaded within 24 hours, prepare the following regulatory onboarding documents:

1. Active MC / DOT Authority

Valid operating credentials must be registered with FMCSA safely.

2. Certificate of Insurance (COI)

Naming HFDS as certificate holder ($1,000,000 Auto-Liability & $100,000 Cargo standard).

3. Signed W-9 Form

Must be completed accurately, signed, and updated within the current year.

4. Signed Dispatch Agreement

Our operational mandate document outlining billing expectations and rules.

5. Factoring Information (If Applicable)

Notice of Assignment sheets so your payouts hit clear accounts automatically.
